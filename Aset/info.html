Ferrari


Ferrari is a super car manufacturer and high-performance Italian racing car based in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, as "Scuderia Ferrari", a company that sponsors racers and makes racing cars before moving to the production of commercial vehicles known as Ferrari in 1947. Throughout its history, this company has participated the longest in the world of racing , especially in Formula One, where it had been a big success.
